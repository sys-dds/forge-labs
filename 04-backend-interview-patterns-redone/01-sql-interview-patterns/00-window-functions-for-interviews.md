# Window Functions For Interviews

Clinics 014, 015, 016, and 018 show the practical window patterns interviewers keep returning to: cumulative sums, rolling frames, ranking buckets, and session groups. Inspect `ledger_entries` in 014 for same-timestamp tie-breaks, `daily_listing_impressions` in 015 for zero rows and skipped dates, `creators` in 016 for trust-adjusted scoring, and `feed_events` in 018 for 30-minute boundaries.
