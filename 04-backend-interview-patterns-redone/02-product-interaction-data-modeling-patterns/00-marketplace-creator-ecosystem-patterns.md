# Marketplace Creator Ecosystem Patterns

Marketplace and creator surfaces need source rows that explain why a buyer, seller, creator, listing, or booking is shown. In these clinics, `MegaMaker` can be capped by `exposure_events`, `QuietCraft` can receive a long-tail slot from `exposure_budget_windows`, listing `101` can win because it matches Ada's `buyer_intents`, and `RiskSeller` can be reviewed from component rows rather than a vague seller score.

The read contract should preserve the trace: budget remaining, intent components, availability, seller quality components, offer/booking lifecycle, local supply/demand pressure, marketplace risk rows, and treatment reason.
