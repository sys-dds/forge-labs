# Policy Decision And Audit Lineage

Reports do not equal violations. A useful safety model can answer:

- Which evidence rows supported this decision?
- Which treatment was applied?
- Who or what actor applied it?
- Was there an appeal?
- Did the appeal reverse the treatment?
- What audit rows connect report, decision, treatment, and reversal?

The capstone trace in clinic 020 intentionally links report, decision, treatment, appeal, trust component, abuse evidence, and audit rows into one explainable product contract.
