# Buyer Intent Seller Quality And Supply Demand

Buyer intent is a row, not a search box string. Seller quality is a component breakdown, not one opaque score. Supply/demand is a local snapshot, not a global popularity proxy.

Use `buyer_intents`, `listing_availability`, `seller_quality_components`, `seller_trust_components`, `offers`, `bookings`, `local_supply_snapshots`, and `local_demand_snapshots` to explain concrete outcomes: listing `101` selected for Ada, listing `102` suppressed as already seen, pending offer `301` not yet a booking, completed booking `401` consuming historical capacity, and fraud risk event `1701` changing the marketplace treatment.
