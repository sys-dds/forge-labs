# Creator Ecosystem For Beginners

Creator ecosystem means the feed considers Ada, creators, posts, exposure budget, cold-start creator evidence, long-tail content, creator fatigue, and ecosystem health together. In clinic 61, creator_maya has 0 historical exposure and quality 82, so she receives a slot while creator_omar has quality 42 and is suppressed.

Common beginner mistake: treating the most engaged creator as the only relevant creator. Debug it by reading exposure_rows, popularity_bias_rows, and ecosystem debug trace entries for each creator ID. A senior engineer asks which exposure target, author cap, and outcome feedback row changed the final feed.
