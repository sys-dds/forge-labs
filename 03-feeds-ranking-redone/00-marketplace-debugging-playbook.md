# Marketplace Debugging Playbook

Start with buyer intent, then supply, seller quality, local availability, risk suppression, and ecosystem debug trace. Clinic 68 suppresses listing_201 because dispute rate 0.25, refund rate 0.30, fraud_flags 3, and a cheap price anomaly produce high fraud risk. Clinic 67 backfills listing_301 from Edinburgh because Glasgow demand is high and trusted local supply is scarce.

Common beginner mistake: asking why a listing lost only at the final score stage. Debug category, location, budget, availability, response time, cancellation rate, dispute rate, refund rate, fraud risk, local availability, and supply-demand balance in that order. A senior engineer asks which stage owns the treatment and which trace row the support team can quote.
