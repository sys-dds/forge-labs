# Beginner Walkthrough

This clinic solves creator fatigue diversity and repeated-author caps for Ada by comparing user demand with creator or seller supply before final ranking. Beginners often confuse relevance with ecosystem health: the top raw score can be relevant while also overexposed, risky, too repetitive, too far away, or untrusted.

The broken system did this: post_501, post_101, post_102, post_103, post_601 by raw score, including low quality and blocked supply.

The corrected system did this: post_101, post_102, post_201, post_301, post_401 with Ben capped and three plus creators visible.

Rows that prove the lesson: post_101, post_102, post_103, post_201, post_301. A tiny calculation to inspect is `shown / target`: if a creator has 900 shown events against a 300 target, the exposure ratio is 3.0, so the ranker needs a popularity-bias control before filling every slot with the same creator.
