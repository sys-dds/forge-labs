# Beginner Walkthrough

This clinic solves ecosystem-stage missing explanations for Ada by comparing user demand with creator or seller supply before final ranking. Beginners often confuse relevance with ecosystem health: the top raw score can be relevant while also overexposed, risky, too repetitive, too far away, or untrusted.

The broken system did this: All misses are called low_score and creator_maya is omitted despite the exploration result.

The corrected system did this: Shown feed keeps post_101, listing_201, creator_maya and every miss has reason plus stage.

Rows that prove the lesson: post_101, listing_201, creator_maya, post_301, creator_lina. A tiny calculation to inspect is `shown / target`: if a creator has 900 shown events against a 300 target, the exposure ratio is 3.0, so the ranker needs a popularity-bias control before filling every slot with the same creator.
