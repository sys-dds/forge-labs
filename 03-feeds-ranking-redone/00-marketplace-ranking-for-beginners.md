# Marketplace Ranking For Beginners

Marketplace ecosystem means ranking supply for buyer demand in a two-sided marketplace. Clinic 65 starts from buyer intent: sink repair, Glasgow, 8000 pence, today or tomorrow. Listing relevance uses category, location, budget, and availability before price wins.

Seller quality is not a vibe: clinic 66 uses response time, cancellation rate, dispute rate, completed jobs, and fulfilment quality to build a trust score. Common beginner mistake: sorting by rating or cheapest price. Debug it by comparing buyer_intent_rows, seller_quality_rows, trust_score_rows, and risk_score_rows. A senior engineer asks whether the ranking handoff explains every treatment.
