# Roadmap

This path covers feed and recommendation product patterns with deterministic simulations.

Banking Systems Redone, Backend Interview Patterns Redone, and PostgreSQL Redone are separate tracks. Do not move to another track without explicit user approval.

## Foundation Review Gate

Feeds/Ranking clinics 01-10 cover pipeline foundations: inventory, candidate generation, eligibility, scoring, mixing, diversity, stories, notifications, and replay/debugging.

## Product Surface Clinics

FORGE-FEED-R2-011-020 stays on Feeds/Ranking Redone and covers product surface ranking patterns such as Following vs For You, short video, home/up-next, hot ranking, professional feeds, marketplace recommendations, search, creator discovery, safety, and beginner ranking maths.

## Operating Clinics

FORGE-FEED-R2-021-030 stays on Feeds/Ranking Redone and covers evaluation, experiments, feedback loops, incidents, freshness, latency, and senior feed system design.

## Retrieval Clinics

FORGE-FEED-R2-031-040 stays on Feeds/Ranking Redone and covers advanced retrieval, embeddings, ANN concepts, hybrid source blending, recall and precision evaluation, cold-start backfill, missing-candidate debugging, and senior retrieval system design.

## Possible Future FORGE-FEED-R2-041-050 Options

Future feed work can deepen social graph and community algorithms, trust and safety ranking, abuse/integrity retrieval, and creator ecosystem or marketplace depth. Do not create that range until the user asks for it.
